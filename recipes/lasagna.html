Lasagna

The perfect Italian Lasagna recipe. An authentic bolognaise, bechamel and home-made noodles combine to create an icon of food.

Ingredients

  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Variety of cheese - parmesan, mozzarella
  • Home-made lasagna sheets
  • Bechamel Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook red sauce
  2. Cook white sauce
  3. Cook lasagna sheets
  4. Layer with care, top with cheese and bake
  5. Eat!
