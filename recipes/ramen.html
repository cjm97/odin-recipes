Ramen

A classic, quick university/college staple meal.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth
  • Any number of condiments you'd like - meat, vegetables, it's a free for all.

Steps

  1. Cook your ramen noodles
  2. Heat up your broth, and pour it into a bowl
  3. Layer your noodles into your broth - don't cook your noodles in the broth, it can ruin the texture.
  4. Add your condiments
